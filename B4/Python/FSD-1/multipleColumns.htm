Pushpa' star Allu Arjun beats Shah Rukh Khan, Thalapathy Vijay, Prabhas and others to become the highest-paid actor in India: Report

In the Indian film industry, actors' pay is often tied to their popularity, with rates soaring after blockbuster hits. Recently, one actor's fees have sparked major buzz, as reports suggest they are now charging over Rs 100 crore for a film, making them the highest-paid actor in the country.
